How it works

  • Each source pushes streamlines outward, while each sink pulls them inward.
  • The drawn curves follow the local velocity direction, just like electric field lines follow the electric field direction.
  • Near a source or sink, the speed grows, so the tracing routine shortens steps near singular points to stay stable.
