Interaction
Click Add source or Add sink, then drag any point to a new location. Streamlines are drawn tangent to the velocity field, just like field lines are tangent to an electric field, but here the vectors represent ideal fluid flow from sources and into sinks.
  1. Sources push streamlines outward.
  2. Sinks pull streamlines inward.
  3. A source-sink pair forms a dipole-like flow pattern.
